FAQ

Is it free?

Yes — 100% free and open source.

Which browsers are supported?

Cookie Editor Pro targets Chrome (Manifest V3). It also works on Edge and other Chromium-based browsers.

Does it send my cookies anywhere?

No. All data stays local in your browser. The extension has no analytics, trackers, or remote servers.
